Network Relevance

Without network monitoring and alerting, lateral movement by attackers and anomaly patterns remain unseen, delaying detection and response. Organizations without proper logging have an average breach detection time of 287 days, compared to 27 days for those with mature security monitoring. This allows attackers to persist in networks, exfiltrate data, and establish multiple backdoors before detection.
